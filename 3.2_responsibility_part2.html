The kind of responsibility a judge has is very different from the kind of responsibility that a criminal or a lock-pick-maker has. The better way to think of this is that the AI governance actor is responsible for holding responsible the actor who is properly responsible. It would be recursive that AI governance actor is themselves responsible.
